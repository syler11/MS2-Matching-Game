Pair the animals

Memory game for kids

How to Play:

Complete the game by finding all the matching pairs of animal cards.

First select the difficulty of the game then select two cards by clicking on the back of the card.

If they do not match, the cards will flip back to their backs.

If they do match, the cards will remain face up.

Keep going until you turn all the animal cards!
